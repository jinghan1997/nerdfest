Visions

Hardware training

  • Train people to design and build MRI hardware — not just theories.
  • Brainstorm strategies for portable MRI challenges.

Access & impact

  • Promote portable MRI for resource-limited and/or point-of-care settings.
  • Enhance MRI accessibility by lowering barriers worldwide.

Global community

  • Build a global community of engineers and researchers who share the same vision.

We offer

  • Expert seminars — training, workshops, and poster exhibitions.
  • Mentorship from world-class experts and hardware specialists.
  • Lab access — equipment, tools, and workspace for your team.

Your role

  • Learn
  • Form or join a team
  • Contribute to a working MRI scanner

Building together, solving a big picture. Each team focuses on a critical subsystem. Together, all combine to form a complete portable MRI.
