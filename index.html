Themes

Building together, solving a big picture. Each team focuses on a critical subsystem. Together, all combine to form a complete portable MRI.

How Does It Work?

portableMRI NerdFest-2 is built around eight technical themes, each representing a key subsystem of an open-source portable MRI system. Participants join a theme, work in small teams, and contribute toward a final integrated demonstration.

1

Choose a theme

Participants state their preferred technical themes. Assignments prioritize first choices, then second choices, while keeping team sizes balanced.

2

Join a track

Each theme has Beginner and Advanced tracks, matched to participants' background, confidence, and prior MRI or engineering experience.

3

Build in teams

Each track may contain multiple teams of about five participants. Teams stay within their theme throughout the event.

4

Integrate the system

Teams develop, test, and validate their subsystem so it can connect with the other themes for the Day 4 system demonstration.

Foundation path

Beginner Track

Designed for participants who are new to MRI or want a stronger foundation in a specific technical area.

  • Lectures and guided tutorials
  • Hands-on practical exercises
  • Core concepts, tools, and essential skills
Challenge path

Advanced Track

Designed for participants with relevant knowledge or experience who want to work directly on harder engineering problems.

  • Subsystem design, optimization, construction, and testing
  • Open-source improvements and new technical ideas
  • Close collaboration with mentors and integration teams
Theme example

From learning to a working subsystem

Console teams develop, implement, and test open-source MRI console hardware and software so the console can control the MRI system and interface correctly with other subsystems.

Magnets & Shimming teams may explore permanent magnet arrays, magnet properties, simulation tools, introductory magnet design, and basic shimming techniques.
